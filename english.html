Biography

Pechorin is the fictional protagonist of Mikhail Lermontov's novel A Hero of Our Time. Pechorin is a negative character. He served in the Caucasus. He was a cold-blooded and strange character. He died in Persia.

Maxim Maksimych - staff captain, Pechorin's friend, is elderly. Maxim Maksimych is uneducated and comes from a poor family.

Bela - the daughter of a Kabardian prince, She falls in love with Pechorin, but he cannot truly love her, and their relationship ends tragically.

Mery - One of the main characters in Mikhail Lermontov's novel "A Hero of Our Time." Slender, fragile, graceful, intelligent, proud.

Olga - An officer's wife and mother of two small children. She writes a farewell letter to Pechorin before leaving. They were together but separated.

Themes

  1. The superfluous man
  2. The meaning of life and inner emptiness
  3. Love as a test
  4. Freedom and Destiny
  5. Egoism and responsibility
  6. Loneliness

Setting and plot

Pyatigorsk and Kislovodsk

Plot- A young officer indulges his own whims in everything, disregarding the feelings of those around him. Innocent people fall victim to his egocentrism, but the hero himself remains disillusioned and lonely.

Why I like this book

Because in this novel, you can compare the main character with himself.
